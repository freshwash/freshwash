FAQ

Common questions

How long does a bin cleaning take? +
Most bin cleanings take about 15 minutes per bin. We handle everything curbside — no need for you to be home.
Do I need to be home during the service? +
Nope. Just leave your bins out (or let us know where to access your patio/siding) and we'll take care of the rest. We'll send you before and after photos when we're done.
What cleaning products do you use? +
We use eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning agents that are safe for your family, pets, plants, and the environment. No harsh chemicals. Have a preference? Let us know!
Do you offer recurring service? +
Yes! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly plans for bin cleaning. Patio and siding are typically seasonal or one-time, but we can set up a schedule that works for you.
What areas do you service? +
We currently service Oakland County. Reach out for a quote and we'll let you know if we cover your neighborhood.
