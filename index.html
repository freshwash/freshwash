FAQ

Common questions

Will pressure washing damage my property? +
Not with us. We adjust pressure based on the surface — high pressure for concrete and pavers, soft wash for siding, wood, and more delicate areas. We know what each surface can handle and treat it accordingly.
Do I need to be home during the service? +
No. As long as we have access to the area being cleaned, you don't need to be there. We'll send you before and after photos once the job is done.
What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing? +
Pressure washing uses high-powered water to blast away heavy buildup — ideal for concrete, pavers, and driveways. Soft washing uses lower pressure paired with a cleaning solution to safely treat more delicate surfaces like siding, wood, and trim. We assess each job and use whichever method — or a combination of both — gets the best result without risking damage.
How often should I have my exterior cleaned? +
It depends on the surface. Patios and siding typically benefit from a cleaning once or twice a year, especially after winter. High-traffic or shaded areas tend to build up grime faster. We're happy to recommend a schedule after seeing your property.
What areas do you service? +
We currently service Oakland County. Reach out and we'll let you know if we cover your neighborhood — we're always expanding.
